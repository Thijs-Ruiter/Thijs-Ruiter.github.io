About me

I'm Thijs Ruiter. I'm currently in my exam year of Dutch high school (VWO). My personal interest lies in game development and computer science.

For the past six years, I have been fortunate enough to take part in the Coderclass at the Metis Montessori Lyceum in Amsterdam. Coderclass is a school subject, in which you get 5 hours of coding lessons a week. With this, I have been able to dip my toes in a lot of coding languages and to get good at the languages I'm interested in (mainly being Unity).

Projects

Scheepvaart Game

As part of a technasium project, I was tasked with 2 others, to design an educational game for the Scheepvaart Museum. We were asked to shed some light on the other perspective of slavery, the perspective of the enslaved people. Thus, we designed a game where you play as an enslaved person and must make decisions that boil down to cooperation or attempt to escape and earn freedom.

Top-down Dungeoncrawler

I made a sortof top down dungeoncrawler style game, in which you traverse rooms of enemies. This project was my first time making a generation algorithm to generate a dungeon. For the aestetics I did use free to use assets online.

C++ Course VU

I got to do a C++ pre-university course from the Vrije Univeriteit in Amsterdam. The course took about 4 months and included all the basics of C++ and more. We were taught things such as vectors, pointers, classes, error catching, avoiding memory leaks and recursive algoritms.
